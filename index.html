Power and Evangelical Global Outreach Ministry

Power and Evangelical Global Outreach Ministry-P.E.G.O Ministry is a ministry commissioned by God to winning of souls and transforming lives through the preaching of the Gosple,working of Miracles and Prophetic intervention. It is a young ministry with the vision of-RAISING A GENERATION OF POWER.

Find Out More

"And all things are of God,who hath reconciled us to himself by Jesus Christ,and hath given to us the ministry of reconciliation"(KJV) 2Corinthians 5:18

The ministry without reserve is into transforming lives,delivering the captives from sin and satanic strongholds;and bringing hope to the destitute. It is fully an outreach ministry under the auspices of the Holy Spirit;situated in Owerri,Imo State Nigeria. With a branch in India. P E G O Ministry is totally an evangelical ministry,a non-denominational ministry working and touching lives with the love of God. The ministry is working passionately to bless lives and reach the unsaved globally through-Crusades,Evangelical Outreaches,Mission Trips,Revival meetings,Love Display to the needy,empowerment program e. t.c. P E G O Ministry just started a Bible School-with the sole aim of equipping church workers and ministers. To enhance believers who have passion for God's work but may not have had opportunity for formal education. Power and Evangelical Global Outreach Ministries is a Prophetic,Deliverance and Healing ministry;it has blessed lives both locally and internationally with amazing testimonies. Together we can reach the whole world with the Love of God and Gosple of the Kingdom Matthew 24:14. Your prayers are needed,your constructive suggestions will do us good,and also your financial assistance and commitnent will be appreciated. "Go ye therefore,and teach all nations,baptizing them in the name of the Father,and of the Son,and of the Holy Ghost"(KJV) Matthew 28:19.

Ministries

EVANGELICAL OUTREACHES

The ministry organizes outreach programs wether in large or small scale,gathering people together to hear from God. Sometimes it is on streets, on open air evangelism. Bringing evangelism to people,at times workers are dispersed in groups to witness to people on the road,shops,markets,through hospital visitation etc Several techniques are followed to make outreach fulfilled.

GOOD SAMARITAN MINISTRY

This is the arm of PEGO Ministry that deals with Love Display to people. The ministry works on taking care of the poor and needy and bringing the Love of God and gosple to the destitute.

DYNAMIC BIBLE SCHOOL

This is the training arm of Power and Evangelical Global Outreach Ministries. The bible school is for equipping church workers and pastors for the work of God. Administering to them on Kingdom Priciples and how to start and build a strong ministry

CHILDREN'S FELLOWSHIP

This is a non-and-inter-denominational arm of the ministry where children are gathered from different churches for bible teachings,bible quiz,prayer meetings and camping program. Children are blessedly taught the love of God,as the gladly listen. Bible quiz and other side attraction helps to bring children together. Age concentration is from infant till Juniour secondary School 3(JSS3)

MISSION TRIPS

PEGO Ministry brings and takes the Gospel to Nations and to the unreached through Mission Trips .The ministry hosts Healing and Revival meetings in other countries and invites pastors for conference and spiritual activities. The ministry sponsors Gospel Mission Trips to other nations.
